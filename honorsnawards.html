2021 Code for Gender Gender Hackathon Finalist, Rank 8th/50 [slides][link}

Issued by Womany X Google · Aug 2021

This is an innovative competition aimed at solving gender-related issues. I decided to pay attention to the trend of decreasing willingness to breastfeed, and started from the perspective of improving feeding tools, and developed a product called Breast Pump APP, which combines Bluetooth control, functional design, and UI design, with the purpose of providing better feeding experience.

Assistant Professor Xu Meng Chao Memorial Scholarship

Issued by National Taiwan University of Science and Technology · Mar 2021

The competition for this scholarship was highly competitive, but I stood out from the many on-campus positions during my studies and successfully won this honor. These include serving as the Co-Founder and Vice President of the NTUST ECE student association, the Residential Assistant of the international student dormitory, and the English Teaching Assistant of the Language Center.

I then used this scholarship to apply for the TOEFL, TOEIC and Taiwan Federation of University Transfer Examinations, and in my sophomore year I transferred to the Department of Electrical Engineering of Tsinghua University with a score of 97.09. This transfer process not only demonstrated my academic abilities but also emphasized my commitment to continuous learning and excellence, as well as my ability to adapt in a different academic environment.
